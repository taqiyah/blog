mulligoon arises

february 3, 2021

upon creating this website/blog/journal thing, i was pretty unsure about what i wanted out of it. i think i now know where i want to go from here.

let me go into this name i chose first. "mulligoon" is basically just a mixture of my little nickname on tik tok - yung looney gooney - and the name of a pretty awesome band - hot mulligan - . the word "mulligan" is kind of silly. it has a few meanings actually. from what i know, it's a golf term where a player is given a free shot due to their previous shot going awry. it's a do-over! google also says that it is a type of stew. but i like the first meaning more. my silly self is giving myself a do-over. a do-over in what, you may ask?

well, in everything, to be honest!

i'm freeing myself from the negativity, the paranoia, the anxiety, the frustration, the anger â€“ all that stuff. at least i'll be trying to. it's not an overnight thing, of course, nor a permanent thing, but it's a start.

so you guys, my readers, are my goonies :) and i will address you all as such.


now that that's aside, let's get into more content-related things.

i don't have a schedule for this, so as a full-time student and part-time worker, i usually have little time to write. i'm just going to post what i feel, when i feel like (since i don't really have regular readers, this is more so just for me to remind myself). right now, i have so many plans for posts. i want to have a little fitness series, a thrift fashion series, more playlist posts, and general updates. i'm pretty excited for the future of my website. it's starting to bloom!

well, goonies, that's my first 2021 update, and i hope you keep checking in for more.
